How to Play Ludo

Objective

Be the first player to move all 4 tokens from your base to your home (finish area).

Game Rules

  • Starting: Roll a 6 to move a token out of your base onto the starting square.
  • Movement: Roll the dice and move one of your tokens by the number shown.
  • Extra Turn: Rolling a 6 gives you an extra turn.
  • Capturing: Landing on an opponent's token (on non-safe squares) sends it back to their base.
  • Safe Squares: Starting squares and colored home lanes are safe from captures.
  • Winning: Move all 4 tokens into your home finish area to win!

Controls

  • Mouse/Touch: Click/tap to roll dice and select tokens.
  • Keyboard: Press Space or Enter to roll dice. Use Tab and Enter to select tokens.
